May, 2006 This study is a thorough investigation of the quality of American geography literacy. Published in May of 2006, it gives us a very useful set of statistics that are metrics of how much geographical and geopolitical education American children are receiving from our education system. November, 2015 Real Clear Politics is the defacto hub where any analyst can go to see an amalgamation of all polls published in the US relating to matters of national and state elections. They are updated many times a day as new polls come in and create a running list of all polls taken. It creates a set of historical data for easy comparison of current circumstance with historical circumstances that one can use to assess trends in primary and general elections at the state and national levels. 4. Pew Forum. http://www.pewforum.org/2009/10/07/mapping-the-global-muslim-population. October 7, 2009 This is the main article I have used to help us understand the Muslim world. It specifically lays out many statistics about differences within the Muslim world, from religion, to ethnicity, to language, to national origin. It is the main basis of my article's thesis: the idea that the Muslim world is much more complicated than many Americans believe it to be. This is a fabulous study that describes and shows in detail how diverse and broad the Muslim population of our planet is.
